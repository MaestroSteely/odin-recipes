Here is a simple recipe to make a classic cup of coffee:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cups of water
  • tablespoons of ground coffee
  • Sugar and milk (optional)

Equipment:

  • Coffee maker or French press
  • Coffee mug

Instructions:

  1. Boil water: Fill the coffee maker or a saucepan with 1 1/2 cups of water and heat it until boiling. If using a French press, wait for the water to cool for about a minute to avoid burning the coffee.
  2. Prepare the coffee grounds: Take 2 tablespoons of ground coffee and place it into the coffee maker's filter or the French press.
  3. Brew the coffee: If using a coffee maker, simply turn it on and let it brew. If using a French press, pour the hot water over the coffee grounds and let it steep for 4 minutes.
  4. Press the French press: If using a French press, slowly press down on the plunger to separate the coffee grounds from the liquid.
  5. Serve the coffee: Pour the coffee into a mug and enjoy it black, or add sugar and milk to taste.

Enjoy your delicious cup of coffee!
