Here is a simple and delicious recipe to make a classic burger at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayo, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and form each portion into a patty that's about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Heat a grill or a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4 minutes on each side or until they reach an internal temperature of 160Â°F.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in the skillet for 1-2 minutes until they're golden brown.
  6. If using cheese, place a slice on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom bun, followed by the toppings of your choice, and the top bun.
  8. Serve the burgers hot and enjoy!
  9. This recipe makes 4 classic burgers, but the ingredients can be easily scaled up or down to make more or fewer burgers.

Enjoy your delicious chicken chilly!
